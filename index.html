What can Tingbot do?

Tingbot is designed for novel and new experiences.

The Tingbot team

About the project

Tingbot is a project by NORD collective, a team of designers and developers who enjoy creating things together.

Initially, we created Tingbot as a fun way to experience Ting.it but it didn't take us long to realise the platform we'd built could be used for all sorts of exciting projects.

On the 25th and 26th of April we will be showing Tingbot in it's current form at Maker Faire UK in Newcastle. If you are planning to go, come and find us!

What comes next?

After Maker Faire, we are keen to make Tingbot available to all of you.

So far, this is how we see things going forward:

Maker Faire UK - a chance to introduce people to Tingbot and invite feedback

Closed Beta kits - the first time others will be let loose with the product outside of our team - this will help us make it the best it can be

Fund raising - a crowdfunding campaign or other method to finance further production, including:
- hardware development (case and components)
- software development (OS, Tide)
- first product release

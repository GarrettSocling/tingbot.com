Tingbot Code of Conduct

Tingbot is dedicated to a safe, inclusive, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone. If you're taking part in our online community (e.g. our GitHub, Slack), you're required to abide by this code of conduct.

Simply put: be considerate, don't be mean, and respect your fellow Tingbotters.

For the avoidance of doubt, examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

  • Trolling or insulting/derogatory comments
  • Personal attacks
  • The use of sexualized language or imagery
  • The use of offensive language (remember some of our users are children)
  • Public or private harassment
  • Publishing other's private information, such as physical or electronic addresses, without explicit permission
  • Other unethical or unprofessional conduct

The Tingbot core team have the right to edit/remove any posts, code, or other contributions that don't align with this code of conduct, and potentially ban any contributor that is repeatedly violating.

Unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the core team at hello at tingbot dot com.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 1.3.0, available from http://contributor-covenant.org/version/1/3/0/
